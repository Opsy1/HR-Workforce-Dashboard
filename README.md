# HR Workforce Analytics Dashboard

### Description
This project delivers a comprehensive HR Workforce Analytics dashboard designed to provide actionable insights into employee data. The primary purpose is to empower management with data-driven decision-making capabilities by visualizing workforce distribution, attrition trends, and employee experience metrics. Through interactive reporting, this solution helps identify key drivers of employee turnover and organizational performance bottlenecks.

### Tech Stack
* Power Query: Employed for data extraction, cleaning, and transformation to ensure high data quality.
* Power BI: Utilized for creating interactive visualizations, building data models, and designing the user interface.

### Data Source
* The dataset was provided by HieLite Academy as part of their professional training program.

### Features & Highlights

#### Business Problem
Organizations often struggle to understand the underlying causes of employee attrition and the demographics of their active workforce. This dashboard addresses these challenges by consolidating disparate HR data into a unified, interactive platform.

#### Goal
The goal of this dashboard is to provide a holistic view of the workforce, enabling HR leaders to:
* Monitor workforce composition (e.g., by department, gender, and age group).
* Analyze attrition rates to identify high-risk departments and job roles.
* Evaluate employee experience factors such as monthly income and work-life balance.

#### Key Visuals & Insights
* **Workforce Overview**: Provides a summary of total employees, active staff, and turnover figures, offering a snapshot of current organizational health.
* **Attrition Analysis**: Features breakdowns of attrition by department, age, gender, job role, and overtime status.
* **Employee Experience**: Visualizes performance ratings, job satisfaction levels, and income distributions across departments.
* **Insights**: Identifying that specific roles (like Laboratory Technicians) or departments (like Sales) may require targeted retention strategies based on higher attrition percentages. 


#### Business Impact
This dashboard transforms raw data into strategic intelligence, allowing the organization to implement proactive retention programs, optimize resource allocation, and improve overall employee satisfaction. To improve employee retention, management should focus on 
strengthening employee engagement, onboarding, mentoring, and career development opportunities.
